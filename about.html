About Our Department

Learn about our history, mission, and academic environment

Our History

The Department of Statistics, Krishnagar Government College, came into existence in 1981. From 2018- 2022 under the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS), the department had been offering only General course in Statistics. This course provides an exciting opportunity to engage with the quantitative aspects of the social, biological and physical sciences.

Generally students from Mathematics, Economics and Physics departments opt for this subject. Since its inception, the department strives hard to maintain its distinctive competence. From the 2023-24 session, both Major and Minor courses in Statistics shall be offered by the department, following the regulations of the University of Kalyani, in compliance with the NEP 2020.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a center of excellence in statistical education and research, contributing to societal development through data-driven solutions.

Mission

To provide high-quality education in statistics, foster research and innovation, and develop analytical thinkers who can contribute meaningfully to various fields.

Facilities & Environment

Computational Labs

Library Resources

Extensive collection of statistical literature, journals, and research databases.

Collaborative Environment

Research Opportunities
