Department of Statistics

Krishnagar Government College, Nadia

Established in 1981, the Department of Statistics at Krishnagar Government College offers a top-tier undergraduate statistics program. Our curriculum provides an exciting opportunity to engage with the quantitative aspects of the social, biological, and physical sciences.
